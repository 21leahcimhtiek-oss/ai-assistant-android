## Marketing Assets

### Screenshots Template

Each app should have 5 screenshots:
1. **Main Feature** - Show the most unique feature (holographic, AI chat, etc.)
2. **Tracking/Analytics** - Show progress visualization
3. **Therapy Connection** - Show video therapy or professional support
4. **Community/Support** - Show community features or resources
5. **Call to Action** - Show signup or premium upgrade

### Preview Video Template

- Duration: 30-60 seconds
- Show app opening and key features
- Include testimonial or success story
- End with call to action
- Include captions for accessibility

### Icon Requirements

- **Size:** 1024x1024 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Safe Area:** 900x900 pixels
- **Background:** Solid color or gradient
- **No rounded corners** (OS will apply)

### Keywords Strategy

- **Primary Keywords:** Main therapy type (e.g., "trauma therapy", "anxiety relief")
- **Secondary Keywords:** Related conditions (e.g., "PTSD", "panic attack")
- **Tertiary Keywords:** Features (e.g., "AI therapist", "video therapy")
- **Long-tail Keywords:** Specific use cases (e.g., "trauma therapy for PTSD")

### Localization

Translate all content to:
- Spanish (es)
- French (fr)
- German (de)
- Japanese (ja)
- Mandarin Chinese (zh-Hans)

## Compliance & Legal

### Privacy Policy

All apps must include:
- Data collection practices
- HIPAA compliance statement
- User rights and data deletion
- Third-party integrations (Stripe, SendGrid, etc.)
- Crisis resources and liability disclaimer

### Content Rating

- **4+:** General wellness apps (MindSpace, AnxietyCalm, SleepWell, StressRelief)
- **12+:** Apps with relationship/grief content (RelationshipAI, GriefCompanion, MoodLift)
- **17+:** Apps with addiction/trauma content (TraumaHeal, AddictionFree)

### Crisis Resources

Every app must include:
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
- Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
- International Association for Suicide Prevention: https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/

## App Store Optimization (ASO)

### Title Strategy

- **Format:** [Main Feature] + [Benefit] + [Unique Angle]
- **Example:** "TraumaHeal - Holographic PTSD Therapy with AI"
- **Length:** 30 characters max for iOS, 50 for Android

### Subtitle Strategy

- **iOS:** 30 characters max
- **Android:** N/A (use short description)
- **Focus:** Key benefit or unique feature

### Description Strategy

- **First 3 lines:** Hook and main benefits
- **Bullet points:** Key features
- **Social proof:** User testimonials or statistics
- **Call to action:** Download or free trial

### Review Management

- Respond to all reviews within 48 hours
- Address negative reviews professionally
- Highlight positive reviews in marketing
- Request reviews after positive user experiences
